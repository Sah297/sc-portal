Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

The April 2025 Iberian blackout exposed critical supply chain fragility across major manufacturing sectors (automotive, food, chemicals) with no backup systems. Spanish and Portuguese governments are launching infrastructure resilience initiatives following Europe's worst blackout in 20+ years, creating urgent demand for supply chain risk management and business continuity solutions. The event's scale and public visibility make this a high-urgency opportunity window.

Signals to Lead With

→ Catastrophic Production Disruption — Automotive plants faced $2.3m/hour downtime with complete 10-hour shutdowns across VW, Ford, Stellantis, Seat, and Iveco facilities creating immediate business continuity urgency.
→ Infrastructure Vulnerability Exposed — 60% of Spain's 25 GW generation lost in 5 seconds with insufficient synchronous backup revealing systemic resilience gaps requiring multi-billion euro grid modernization investments.
→ Regulatory Action Triggered — Spain updated Operational Procedure 7.4 in June 2025 mandating grid-forming capability and ancillary services, driving compliance-driven supply chain investment across affected industries.
→ Cross-Sector Impact Scale — Motor vehicles, food production, and chemicals sectors simultaneously halted with 30,000+ business travelers disrupted daily, demonstrating need for coordinated multi-industry resilience planning.

How Accenture Can Help

• Planning Opportunities: Implement integrated business continuity planning with risk-weighted S&OP processes to model power disruption scenarios and alternative sourcing strategies.
• Platform (o9 Solutions): o9 Digital Brain enables real-time supply chain visibility, predictive risk sensing, and rapid scenario planning for infrastructure disruption events.
• Planning as a Service: Accenture PaaS teams can manage continuous risk monitoring and dynamic contingency planning to rapidly adjust production schedules during grid instability.
• AI & Analytics: AI-powered early warning systems detect grid instability patterns and auto-generate alternative production plans before disruptions cascade.
