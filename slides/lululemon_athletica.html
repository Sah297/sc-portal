Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Lululemon faces acute pressure from $275m tariff costs and 220bps margin compression while managing $1.7bn inventory bloat. With CFO acknowledging multi-quarter lag between tariff impact and mitigation execution, and 40% vendor concentration in tariff-exposed Vietnam, the company requires immediate supply chain redesign and advanced planning capabilities to preserve premium positioning. CEO turnover in January 2026 and visible U.S. market weakness create urgency for transformation partnership.
