Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Lululemon faces acute margin compression with Q4 gross margin collapsing 550 basis points to 54.9% and operating margin down 380 basis points while inventory surged 18% to $1.7bn creating immediate working capital strain. The combination of markdown pressure, Vietnam supplier concentration risk (68% capacity, no long-term contracts), tariff headwinds, and underperforming US business with declining Americas comparable sales creates urgent need for supply chain transformation. With $1.8bn cash and leadership focused on action plans, this is a high-urgency procurement window.
