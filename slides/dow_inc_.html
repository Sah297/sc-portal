Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Dow's January 2026 announcement of $657M FY2025 loss and 4,500 job cuts (on top of 1,500 prior cuts) creates an urgent, time-bound window for supply chain transformation. The Transform to Outperform program explicitly targets $2B EBITDA improvement through automation, digital tools, and operating model simplification—directly aligned with Accenture's planning platform and AI capabilities. With restructuring charges of $1.1-1.5B through 2027 and CEO commitment to 'radical simplification,' Dow must rapidly rebuild planning capabilities amid workforce reduction, creating strong pull for PaaS and technology implementation.
