Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Nemak represents a high-urgency opportunity as a Tier 1 supplier facing immediate distress from automotive restructuring, margin compression, and $85m in reorganization costs. The company's net loss of $116m in 2025 versus $25m profit in 2024, combined with debt reduction imperatives and ICE-to-EV transition complexity, creates a compelling burning platform for supply chain transformation. With existing Kinaxis platform and recent $336m GF Casting acquisition integration underway, Nemak needs immediate planning optimization support.
