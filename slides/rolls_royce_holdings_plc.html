Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Rolls-Royce presents an exceptional buying signal with quantified £150-200m annual supply chain cash impact, ongoing disruptions affecting tier-1 customers like British Airways, and explicit guidance acknowledging parts constraints through 2026. The company is in transformation mode under CEO Erginbilgic, delivering strong profitability (£3.5bn operating profit) but constrained by multi-tier supplier visibility gaps as civil aerospace scales to 550-600 OE deliveries and 1,480-1,550 shop visits in 2026.
