Signals to Lead With

→  Active $1.5bn Manufacturing Optimization Program — First phase underway targeting $1.5bn net savings by 2027 with initial savings expected latter part of 2025, creating immediate need for advanced planning capabilities.

→  Geographic concentration crisis exposed — Rocky Mount tornado disrupted 25% of Pfizer's sterile injectables and 8% of total US hospital supply, requiring urgent multi-site network resilience planning.

→  Margin recovery imperative underway — Gross margin improved from 70.1% (2023) to 74.2% (2024) and targeting 75.8% with continued manufacturing optimization driving planning investment justification.

→  Cost realignment program demonstrates change readiness — Successfully delivered $4bn net cost savings through 2024 with additional $500m targeted for 2025, showing organizational capacity for transformation programs.
