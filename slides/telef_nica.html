Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Telefónica's explicit SEC disclosure of critical supplier concentration risk combined with €2.6bn FCF generation and active contingency planning creates an urgent buying window. The company is simultaneously executing network transformation (5G, fiber expansion to 163m premises) while managing disclosed supply chain vulnerabilities—requiring immediate planning modernization. With o9 Solutions already identified and geopolitical supply tensions escalating, this is a high-urgency transformation opportunity.
