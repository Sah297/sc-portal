Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

RTX presents an urgent, high-value opportunity driven by Pentagon pressure on military readiness, Congressional onshoring mandates, and $218bn defense backlog growth requiring immediate supply chain transformation. The combination of $2.6bn capital investments, munitions framework agreements demanding 40%+ production increases, and visible inventory inefficiency creates a compelling catalyst for large-scale planning modernization.

Signals to Lead With

→ Defense backlog surge creating capacity crunch — Backlog jumped 11% to $218bn while munitions deliveries must increase 40%+ YoY, exposing critical capacity and allocation planning gaps.
→ Congressional mandate for supply chain resilience — Onshoring requirements drive $2.6bn capital investment in domestic production, requiring network redesign and make-vs-buy optimization across critical materials.
→ Inventory bloat despite revenue growth — Inventory climbed 10.8% to $13.0bn while revenue grew 11%, indicating $650m working capital opportunity through better allocation and flow optimization.
→ Framework agreements locking long-term commitments — 7-year munitions agreements for Tomahawk, AMRAAM, SM-6 production require immediate planning transformation to meet contracted delivery acceleration and Pentagon readiness targets.
