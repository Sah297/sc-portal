Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

JBS faces acute planning crisis: three-week strike froze 6,000 cattle/day processing while burning $5.4bn FCF in Q1 2025 from inventory buildup. With cattle at 75-year lows, margins declining 6.3% annually, and 250 facilities across 6 continents, JBS urgently needs advanced S&OP, workforce scenario planning, and AI-driven capacity optimization—precisely Accenture's Blue Yonder and planning transformation strengths.

Signals to Lead With

→ Active strike resolution with resumed operations — Greeley strike ended April 5, 2026 after three weeks; management now focused on ramping operations and preventing future disruptions worth 7% of US beef capacity.

→ Complex multi-continent operations under pressure — 250+ facilities across 6 continents processing multiple proteins with cattle shortage and labor volatility require sophisticated integrated planning platform.
