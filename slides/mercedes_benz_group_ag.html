Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Mercedes is in acute crisis mode with 57% profit decline and $1.2bn tariff hit creating immediate need for supply chain transformation. The company just announced aggressive 10% production cost reduction target and network rationalization to 2.2m capacity by 2028, signaling major planning transformation underway. With existing Kinaxis platform, strong buying signal for Accenture to accelerate roadmap and deliver tariff optimization, supplier risk, and margin recovery capabilities.

→ Tariff-driven margin collapse — €1bn ($1.2bn) tariff costs drove adjusted RoS from 8.1% to 5.0% in 2025, with company absorbing costs rather than passing to consumers, creating unsustainable margin pressure.
