Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Mercedes withdrew 2025 guidance in April due to tariff uncertainty, signaling acute planning crisis. With €3.9bn EBIT decline, 450bps margin erosion, and semiconductor supply disruptions, the company urgently needs scenario-based planning capabilities to restore investor confidence and operational control.
