Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Leonardo presents a strong buying signal with ATR targeting 20% delivery uplift in 2026 after missing 2025 targets due to supply chain constraints. With €20.9bn orders (book-to-bill 1.2x) and aerostructures JV integration coming, Leonardo needs supply chain transformation to execute growth without repeating 2025 delivery failures.
