Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Tesco faces acute supply chain vulnerability after the January 2026 Daventry depot blockade disrupted critical post-holiday restocking and the May 2025 Peter Green ransomware attack exposed third-party logistics fragility. With £69.9bn revenue, operating on razor-thin 7.5% margins, and facing £510m cost inflation, Tesco urgently needs resilient planning capabilities to prevent future disruptions that directly impact shelf availability and customer service during peak periods.

Signals to Lead With

→ Recent physical disruption event — Farmers blockaded Tesco's major Daventry distribution hub on 5 January 2026 during critical post-Christmas restocking, demonstrating acute vulnerability to activist supply chain disruption.
→ Third-party cyber incident exposure — Peter Green Chilled ransomware attack on 14 May 2025 halted order processing for chilled food logistics, affecting Tesco deliveries and exposing supplier cyber risk gaps.
→ Cost inflation pressure — Tesco absorbed £510m net operating cost inflation in FY2024/25, requiring continuous efficiency improvements while maintaining service levels under supply chain stress.
→ Existing Blue Yonder relationship — Tesco already uses Blue Yonder platform, creating natural expansion opportunity for advanced modules (risk analytics, control tower, scenario planning) with minimal switching cost.

How Accenture Can Help

• Planning Opportunities: Upgrade S&OP processes to integrate risk-based scenario planning, alternate routing protocols, and multi-tier supplier visibility to mitigate physical/cyber disruption.
• Platform (Blue Yonder): Blue Yonder's network optimization and real-time visibility capabilities enable Tesco to dynamically reroute inventory during depot blockades and cyber incidents.
• Planning as a Service: PaaS model provides rapid deployment of advanced planning capabilities for disruption management without long Blue Yonder implementation cycles.
• AI & Analytics: AI agents monitor real-time disruption signals (protests, cyber threats) and auto-generate alternate fulfillment plans, reducing manual response time from days to hours.
