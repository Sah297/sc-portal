Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

RTX faces an active crisis with $6-7bn total GTF recall costs and $1bn annual compensation payments that directly threaten margin targets and customer relationships. The 5-6x shop visit turnaround overruns and 650+ grounded aircraft create immediate urgency for advanced planning capabilities, and Kinaxis RapidResponse is already their platform, making this a natural expansion opportunity for Accenture to drive rapid value.

Signals to Lead With

→ Active financial distress from quality crisis — RTX took a $3bn Q3 2023 charge with $6-7bn total costs and paid $1bn in compensation in 2025 alone, creating board-level pressure for immediate supply chain intervention.
→ Existing Kinaxis platform creates low-risk entry — RTX already uses Kinaxis RapidResponse, eliminating technology risk and enabling Accenture to focus on rapid implementation of advanced planning capabilities for the GTF recall.
→ Multi-year remediation program through 2028 — With 1,200 engines requiring removal through 2026 and new forging press not online until 2028, RTX needs sustained planning expertise for 3+ year engagement.
→ Customer impact driving external pressure — Airbus is pursuing damages for delayed deliveries and missed 75-jets-per-month target pushed to 2027, creating external forcing function for supply chain transformation.

How Accenture Can Help

• Planning Opportunities: Implement integrated S&OP connecting quality control, repair capacity, spare parts inventory, and customer commitments to eliminate 5-6x shop visit overruns and reduce compensation costs.
• Platform (Kinaxis RapidResponse): Kinaxis RapidResponse enables constraint-based planning for repair capacity, real-time visibility into engine removals, and scenario planning for the multi-year GTF remediation program.
• Planning as a Service: PaaS model provides rapid deployment of expert planners to manage the complex 1,200-engine recall program across quality, capacity, spares, and customer impact scenarios.
• AI & Analytics: AI predicts which engines are at-risk for powder metal defects and optimizes repair shop scheduling to minimize turnaround time and aircraft groundings.
