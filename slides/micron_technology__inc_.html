Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Micron presents exceptional buying signals with entire 2026 HBM capacity sold out and structural supply shortages extending into 2027, requiring immediate planning transformation. The company is investing $20bn CapEx in FY2026 while managing severe allocation tradeoffs where every HBM bit consumes 3x standard DRAM capacity, creating urgent need for constraint-based optimization. With 41% gross margins and data center revenue exceeding 50% of total revenue, sophisticated capacity allocation planning can unlock significant margin expansion during this multi-year supercycle.
