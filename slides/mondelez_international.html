Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Mondelez is in the midst of unprecedented margin compression (580 bps decline) from cocoa inflation while navigating volume declines of 3.7% and cautious 2026 guidance of flat to 2% growth. The CEO explicitly cited soft consumer sentiment, commodity volatility, and geopolitical uncertainty, and the company is implementing portfolio optimization strategies. This is a perfect storm requiring immediate supply chain planning transformation—made more urgent by their ongoing ERP implementation (completing 2028) which creates timing for parallel planning platform deployment.

Signals to Lead With

→ Gross margin compression of 580 bps — Adjusted gross margin dropped from 37.6% (FY2024) to 32.0% (FY2025) despite 8% pricing, driven by cocoa cost inflation and unfavorable product mix.
→ Volume decline of 3.7% in FY2025 — Organic revenue driven entirely by pricing as volume/mix declined 3.7% for full year, with Q4 at -4.8%, signaling consumer elasticity breaking point.
→ Cautious 2026 guidance: flat to 2% growth — Company expects flat to 2% organic revenue growth and flat to 5% adjusted EPS growth for 2026, well below historical 3-5% revenue algorithm.
→ ERP transformation in progress until 2028 — Company is implementing multi-year ERP System Implementation program with completion by 2028, creating window for complementary planning platform deployment on o9.
