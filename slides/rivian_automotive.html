Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Rivian faces acute vulnerability to DRAM shortage with UBS identifying them as more exposed than traditional OEMs due to centralized computing architecture requiring 6x more memory chips. With 70-100% DRAM price increases hitting in 2026 and production guidance down 17%, the company needs immediate supply chain transformation to protect the path to profitability. The $6.6B DOE loan and VW joint venture create urgency to prove operational excellence.
