Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Skoda presents exceptional timing: strong standalone performance (€30.1bn revenue, 8.3% ROS) colliding with existential parent company crisis (VW 44% profit collapse, 50k job cuts, 4 plant closures). This creates urgent need to decouple supply chain planning from VW volatility while protecting Central European supplier ecosystem built around combined volumes. Board already investing in AI and efficiency programs, signaling openness to transformative supply chain modernization.

→ Parent company existential crisis — VW Group 44% profit collapse to €6.9bn, 50,000 job cuts, and potential closure of 4 German plants (Emden, Hanover, Zwickau, Neckarsulm) creates supply chain contagion risk across Skoda's tier 1-2 supplier base.
