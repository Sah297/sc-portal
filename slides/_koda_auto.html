Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Škoda's repeated production suspensions due to semiconductor and Ukraine cable harness disruptions, despite record revenue of €30.1bn in FY2025, signal urgent need for resilient supply planning. The company lost 250,000 units in 2021 (€5bn impact) and continues facing supply volatility. As VW Group's supply chain harmonization lead since 2025, Škoda is positioned to champion enterprise-wide planning transformation, making this a high-urgency, high-value opportunity.

Signals to Lead With

→ Production suspension crisis — Multiple production halts affecting 5 models (Octavia, Enyaq, Fabia, Scala, Kamiq) with 250,000 unit shortfall in 2021 worth €5bn revenue loss.
