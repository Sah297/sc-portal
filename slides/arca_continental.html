Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Arca Continental faces an urgent regulatory shock with IEPS tax nearly doubling in 2026, creating immediate need for sophisticated revenue growth management and supply chain reoptimization. The company achieved record 20.5% EBITDA margin in 2024 but now confronts 3-5% volume decline projections, requiring rapid deployment of planning capabilities to protect margins while navigating competitive intensity. With o9 Solutions already as their platform, this represents a prime expansion opportunity for Accenture to layer AI-driven RGM and network optimization services.
