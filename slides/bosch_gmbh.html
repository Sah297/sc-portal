Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Bosch announced September 2025 it must cut €2.5bn annual costs and double profit to €7bn within two years while managing historic 22,000 job reductions. Operating margin collapsed to 2% in FY2025 with target of 7% now delayed to 2027. This creates urgent need for planning transformation to navigate mobility restructuring, optimize capacity amid demand collapse, and accelerate margin recovery—a perfect storm requiring immediate external expertise.
