Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Boeing completed acquisition of Spirit AeroSystems on December 8, 2025, creating urgent integration imperative to stabilize commercial production and supply chain. With massive forward losses, operating margin of -32%, and fragile supplier network, Spirit requires immediate planning transformation to synchronize with Boeing operations and recover profitability.
