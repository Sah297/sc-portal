Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

General Mills presents a high-urgency opportunity as the company explicitly acknowledges operational framework improvements are needed to restore volume-driven growth after two consecutive years of organic sales decline. With adjusted operating profit down 7% in constant currency and margins compressed 90bps despite industry-leading cost savings, leadership is publicly committing to transform planning capabilities across their Remarkable Experience Framework. The $2.9B operating cash flow and continued divestiture activity ($2.1B yogurt sale) signal capital availability for transformation investments.
