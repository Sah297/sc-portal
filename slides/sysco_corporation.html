Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Sysco faces a critical 24-month window to demonstrate successful integration and deleveraging to credit agencies while under regulatory scrutiny. The $21bn debt load, declining volumes, and margin compression create urgent need for supply chain excellence to unlock $300m+ synergies and restore investor confidence. This is a transformational moment where execution risk meets major upside potential.
