Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Victoria's Secret faces an acute, quantified crisis with $85-100m tariff impact actively compressing margins while attempting growth. The company has publicly acknowledged the need for aggressive supply chain mitigation and is under investor scrutiny following weak guidance. This creates immediate demand for advanced planning capabilities to model tariff scenarios, optimize sourcing, and protect profitability without sacrificing customer pricing.
