Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

PepsiCo's public brand portfolio impairments, $184m Quaker recall charges, and restructuring plan extended to 2030 signal active strategic realignment creating urgent need for S&OP and planning platform transformation. With $5.57bn inventory up 4.4% YoY despite volume pressure and ongoing divestiture activity, the company needs integrated planning capabilities to execute portfolio rationalization while releasing working capital and protecting margins during transition.
