Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Metro presents a high-urgency opportunity as they exit a $1bn supply chain transformation with explicit management commitment to extract efficiency gains in FY2025. With margins compressed to 2-4% and intense competitive pressure from Walmart and Loblaw, Metro must operationalize their new automated fresh facilities immediately to justify the investment and protect market position.
