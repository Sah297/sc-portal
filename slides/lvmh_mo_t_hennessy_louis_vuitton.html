Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

LVMH faces acute planning pressure as Fashion & Leather Goods margins decline 13% and Asia revenue share contracts 2%, yet operating cash flow increased 8% to €11.3bn. Management cited geopolitical uncertainties and supply chain risks for 2026 outlook, signaling readiness for transformation. With €80.8bn revenue and 75+ brands requiring coordinated planning, LVMH presents a marquee $25m+ supply chain platform engagement.
