Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

LVMH faces unprecedented judicial and reputational pressure following Loro Piana's July 2025 court administration for labor exploitation, making this a critical intervention moment. With 2,400 audits conducted, 100 suppliers severed, and operating margins declining 200 bps to 23.1% in 2024, leadership urgently needs enterprise-wide supply chain transformation to protect €84.7bn revenue base and premium brand equity across 70 Maisons.

→ Active judicial administration and court-mandated remediation — Loro Piana under 1-year court oversight with administrator monitoring supply chain improvements, creating forcing function for LVMH-wide transformation investment.
