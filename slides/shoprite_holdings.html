Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Shoprite's H1 2026 results show acute distress with gross margin declining 10 bps to 23.8% amid deflation pricing strategy that drove internal inflation to 0.7% vs 4.7% official rate. The completed Ghana exit (Nov 2025) and Malawi wind-down create transformation window as Blue Yonder platform owner needs planning reinvention to operationalize simplified footprint and recover margins.
