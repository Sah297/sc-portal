Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

General Mills presents a high-urgency opportunity with Q3 FY26 results showing 52% net earnings decline, $600m operating cash deterioration, and explicit acknowledgment that weather-related supply chain disruptions hurt results. The company is in active transformation mode ($122m restructuring charges Q3, 1,400 job cuts announced, plant closures) and has publicly committed to accelerating digital supply chain investments to drive HMM savings of 4%+ in FY27, creating a clear buying window for Accenture's planning and AI capabilities.
