Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Apple faces an urgent DRAM shortage crisis with 90-95% price surges and HBM sold out through 2026, directly threatening Q1 FY2026 product launches that generate peak revenues and margins. With $416bn revenue, 46.9% gross margin, and only 9 inventory days, Apple has zero buffer and must optimize allocation immediately. The company's reliance on single-source suppliers for custom components amplifies risk, creating a compelling need for constraint-based planning and supplier collaboration platforms.

Signals to Lead With

→ Critical material shortage with allocation-only supply — DRAM prices surged 90-95% in Q1 2026 with HBM completely sold out through end-2026, forcing Apple into rationing mode.

→ Zero inventory buffer amplifies urgency — Apple operates at 9 inventory days vs. industry average 93 days; any supply disruption immediately halts production with no safety stock.
