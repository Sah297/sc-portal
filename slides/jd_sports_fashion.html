Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

JD Sports faces a perfect storm of margin compression, tariff exposure, and weak like-for-like sales with profit declining £71m to £923m in FY25. The company is managing £1.9bn in inventory across newly acquired Hibbett and Courir operations while navigating 20-46% tariffs on Vietnam sourcing and 37% on Bangladesh. With £100m share buyback initiated and operational complexity from acquisitions, the CFO and supply chain leadership need immediate planning transformation to stabilize margins, optimize working capital, and build tariff-resilient sourcing strategies—creating a high-urgency window for Accenture's o9-based supply chain reinvention.
