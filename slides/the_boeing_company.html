Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

With negative operating margins forecasted at -7.5% to -8% in early 2026 and $87bn inventory, Boeing requires immediate supply chain transformation support, creating a compelling multi-year engagement opportunity for Target Operating Model redesign and Kinaxis implementation.
