Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Qualcomm faces a perfect storm of supply chain urgency: memory shortages driving customer inventory reductions, margin compression despite record revenue, and $5bn Apple revenue cliff in 2027. The $600m Q2 guidance miss and cautious customer behavior signal immediate need for advanced planning capabilities to stabilize demand volatility and protect margins.
