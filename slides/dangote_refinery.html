Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Dangote Refinery represents an urgent, high-value opportunity with $3.74bn in avoidable costs and 74% capacity underutilization driven by feedstock supply chain dysfunction. The planned $5bn IPO in August 2026 creates a 90-day window where supply chain performance directly impacts valuation, investor confidence, and $40-50bn enterprise value story. With $3.65bn debt requiring operational cash flow and expansion to 1.4M bpd capacity announced, fixing feedstock planning is mission-critical for both immediate operations and future growth.

→ IPO imminent August 2026 — Planned $5bn IPO at $40-50bn valuation in 10 weeks creates immediate pressure to demonstrate operational efficiency and resolve crude supply constraints that currently limit capacity to 26-31%.
