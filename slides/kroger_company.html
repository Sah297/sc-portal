Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Kroger is experiencing acute margin compression in 2026 that annual planning cycles cannot address—LIFO charges up 121% YoY and OG&A rates expanding despite productivity efforts. The $200m working capital swing from defensive forward buys demonstrates planning system inadequacy. With Blue Yonder already deployed, Kroger needs rapid platform extension plus managed services to navigate tariff volatility, making this a high-urgency, large-scale planning transformation opportunity perfectly aligned with Accenture's Target Operating Model and Planning Platform Reinvention campaigns.

Signals to Lead With

→ LIFO charge acceleration — LIFO charges increased from $66m to $146m YTD (121% increase) in FY2025, indicating cost inflation outpacing planning system response speed.
