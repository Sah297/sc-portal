Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Dangote Refinery is Africa's largest IPO planned for Q4 2026 at $25-30bn valuation, creating massive urgency to demonstrate operational excellence and resolve the crude supply constraint that limits utilization to 93.6%. With strong government backing, expansion to 1.4m bpd by 2028-2030, and export momentum to Europe/Africa, solving feedstock planning is mission-critical pre-IPO and creates immediate executive sponsorship for supply chain transformation.

Signals to Lead With

→ IPO timing pressure Q4 2026 — Africa's largest IPO targeting $2.5-3bn equity raise at $25-30bn valuation requires demonstrating operational excellence and resolving 6.4% capacity underutilization before public listing in 4-5 months.
