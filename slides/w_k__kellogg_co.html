Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

The September 2025 Ferrero acquisition creates an immediate, high-urgency window for Accenture intervention. WK Kellogg faces a $230-270m restructuring program through 2027 while Ferrero must integrate this $2.7bn business into $11.4bn of prior North American acquisitions. With revenue declining 7.5% in H1 2025, unplanned supply chain downtime, and a suspended 500bps margin improvement target, the new owner needs rapid supply chain transformation expertise to de-risk integration and unlock synergies.
