Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Iberdrola lost 673,000 contracts over 15 months despite market leadership, creating urgent need for supply chain planning transformation in customer acquisition and retention. With Power & Customers EBITDA down 10% and €177m in unplanned ancillary costs, the company faces immediate pressure to deploy advanced planning and AI to stabilize its customer base and restore margins. The telemarketing ban implementation has disrupted traditional acquisition channels, demanding new data-driven planning capabilities now.

Signals to Lead With

→ Massive customer churn acceleration — 673,000 total contract losses across 2025-Q1 2026 with Q1 2026 showing 39,000 electricity contract losses indicate broken demand planning and retention processes.
→ Power & Customers margin collapse — Power and customers segment EBITDA declined 10% year-over-year to €7,872 million driven by lower wholesale prices and cost overruns requiring immediate cost-to-serve optimization.
→ Unplanned ancillary cost shock — €177 million in non-recurrent ancillary services costs in Iberia exposed lack of contingency planning and supplier collaboration capabilities.
→ Channel disruption from regulation — Telemarketing ban implementation coincided with accelerated churn from 634,000 in 2025 to ongoing Q1 2026 losses forcing urgent digital channel and retention planning transformation.

How Accenture Can Help

• Planning Opportunities: Integrated S&OP connecting customer demand signals, pricing strategy, and network capacity planning to reduce ancillary cost volatility and align retention initiatives with margin-positive segments.
• Platform (Kinaxis RapidResponse): Kinaxis RapidResponse enables real-time scenario planning across customer acquisition, retention forecasting, and supply-demand balancing to respond to telemarketing ban impacts and competitive churn dynamics.
• Planning as a Service: Managed S&OP and demand planning services to continuously optimize customer portfolio mix, churn mitigation tactics, and ancillary cost management across Iberian and UK markets.
• AI & Analytics: AI-driven churn prediction identifies at-risk contracts for proactive retention while demand sensing optimizes pricing and promotional spend to acquire high-value customers and reduce ancillary service costs.
