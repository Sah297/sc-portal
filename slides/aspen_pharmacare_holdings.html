Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Aspen is in acute distress with a $61m net loss, 79% operating profit decline, and CEO acknowledging need to recover lost manufacturing profitability by FY2027. The company faces a perfect storm of underutilized facilities, R4.1bn impairments, 42% HEPS decline, and working capital bloat at 50% of revenue. With JPMorgan upgrading to Neutral on expected recovery and management targeting double-digit FY2026 earnings growth, this is a critical window for Accenture to position as the transformation partner for manufacturing optimization, working capital release, and integrated planning across their restructured footprint.
