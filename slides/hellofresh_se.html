Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

HelloFresh is in acute operational distress with €25m weather headwinds, 7.7% revenue decline, 1.4% AEBITDA margin compression, S&P credit downgrade to junk status, and active facility consolidations cutting 273+ jobs. Management reaffirmed need for operational efficiency improvements and is implementing €300m cost reduction program while struggling with demand volatility and last-mile execution. This creates immediate buying window for supply chain transformation with quantifiable ROI tied to margin recovery and resilience.
