Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

HelloFresh is in active restructuring mode with market exits, declining volumes, and a €300m efficiency reset program 80% complete but struggling to convert cost savings into growth. With new CFO Fabien Simon (started Sept 2025) and Blue Yonder already in place, this is prime territory for Accenture planning transformation and AI analytics engagements.
