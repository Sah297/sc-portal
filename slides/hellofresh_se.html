Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

HelloFresh is experiencing acute operational distress with AEBITDA margin at 1.4% and severe weather exposing supply chain fragility causing €25m impact. The company is undergoing deliberate transformation with existing Blue Yonder platform but clearly struggling to operationalize resilience and planning sophistication. With FY2026 guidance of €375-425m AEBITDA (below FY2025's €422.8m) and continued revenue decline, there's an urgent window for planning modernization and AI-driven risk mitigation before investor confidence erodes further.
