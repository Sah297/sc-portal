Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Cargill faces immediate burning platform: moratorium exit effective January 2026 removes collective deforestation monitoring just as EUDR enforcement begins end-2026, jeopardizing 16% of Brazilian soy exports to EU. With Brazil operations recovering to profitability ($340m profit vs $1.7bn loss) and 51m tonne volume targets, Cargill must build individual traceability infrastructure within 6-9 months or lose market access and sustainability-focused customers (Carrefour, Tesco, Walmart). This is time-bound regulatory forcing function with quantified financial exposure.
