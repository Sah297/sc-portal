Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Stellantis is in crisis mode with new CEO Antonio Filosa executing a thorough reorganization after €22bn in H2 2025 charges and hiring 2,000 engineers mainly in North America. The company explicitly acknowledged quality deterioration from past operational choices and is resetting manufacturing and quality management processes, creating an immediate window for large-scale planning transformation tied to their existing Kinaxis platform.

Signals to Lead With

→ New leadership mandate for transformation — CEO Filosa announced global manufacturing reorganization and hired 2,000 engineers in 2025, signaling executive commitment to operational reset.
