Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Toyota's March 2026 order suspension for hybrid SUVs due to component shortages and $1.9bn North America operating loss represents an urgent planning crisis. With 9-month delivery backlogs and electronic parts instability affecting their highest-demand products, Toyota needs immediate supply chain transformation to prevent further margin erosion and market share loss.
