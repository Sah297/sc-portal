Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Bayer's €3.76bn Q4 2025 loss and aggressive turnaround mandate create urgent need for supply chain transformation. With CEO Bill Anderson targeting €800m annual savings by 2026 through Dynamic Shared Ownership operating model, plus visible inventory and margin compression across Consumer Health and Pharmaceuticals, Bayer faces immediate pressure to stabilize operations. The combination of retailer destocking, FX headwinds, and litigation-driven cash constraints creates a compelling window for Accenture to position integrated planning capabilities.
