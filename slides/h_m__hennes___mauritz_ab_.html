Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

H&M is explicitly pivoting from logistics CapEx to technology investment after years of heavy supply chain spend, creating a prime buying window. With inventory at 134 days vs. Inditex <90, gross margin under markdown pressure (230bps drop Q1 FY25), and management acknowledging the need for AI-driven precision and supply-demand alignment, there is urgent executive appetite for transformation. The o9 platform is already in place, making planning maturity and AI analytics the natural next step.

Signals to Lead With

→ Technology pivot after logistics CapEx — H&M shifted FY26 CapEx down to SEK 9-10bn from 11-12bn in FY25, reallocating from supply chain to tech infrastructure and AI-driven decision-making per CEO statement.
