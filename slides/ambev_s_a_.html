Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Ambev faces an urgent commodity cost crisis with publicly disclosed 4.5%-7.5% COGS inflation for FY2026, primarily aluminum-driven, threatening a hard-won 3-year margin expansion streak. With Q4'25 gross margin already contracting 230 bps and volumes down 1.8%, leadership needs immediate planning capabilities to model commodity scenarios, optimize hedging strategies, and rebalance portfolio mix to protect ~$85m in margin value per 50 bps saved. This is a time-sensitive executive priority with board-level visibility.

Signals to Lead With

→ Public COGS inflation guidance — Ambev issued Material Fact Notice in February 2026 projecting 4.5%-7.5% Cash COGS per hectoliter increase driven by aluminum, signaling board-level urgency.
→ Margin contraction reversal — Q4'25 gross margin contracted 230 bps to 52.6% after 3 consecutive years of expansion, creating executive pressure to reverse the trend.
→ Volume headwinds intensifying — FY2025 volumes declined 1.8% (181.9m hl) due to weather and macro pressures, reducing fixed cost absorption and amplifying margin risk.
→ Strategic margin expansion ambition — Management reaffirmed commitment to consolidated margin expansion in 2026 despite cost pressures, requiring advanced planning tools to deliver.
