Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Dollar General faces active supply chain distress disclosed in 10-K with concrete evidence of $232m portfolio charges and supplier delivery volatility affecting store expansion. The company recently amended credit facility covenants (March 2025) signaling financial pressure, yet posted strong FY2025 recovery with 110 bps margin expansion and improved OTIF metrics (470 bps improvement). This inflection point creates urgent need for planning transformation to sustain momentum, de-risk $1.2bn capital investment in stores and DCs, and address ongoing supplier diversification challenges.
