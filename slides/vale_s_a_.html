Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Vale faces acute logistics distress with 84% vessel delays, $555m in port-related losses, and $1.7bn FCF deterioration from working capital expansion. With $16.8bn inventory, 62 days holding period, and Brazil's structural 15.5% GDP logistics costs, the urgency is immediate. Vale's integrated 2,000km rail-port network requires synchronized planning capabilities that Kinaxis RapidResponse uniquely delivers for mining-to-export orchestration.
