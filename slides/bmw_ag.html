Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

BMW faces converging regulatory and financial pressures creating urgent planning transformation needs. The German supply chain law complaint exposes compliance gaps requiring immediate supplier visibility overhaul, while 2025 guidance shows tariffs cutting margins 100bps and inventory crisis locking $2.8bn working capital. This triple threat of legal exposure, margin compression, and cash constraint creates board-level urgency for rapid planning capability deployment.
