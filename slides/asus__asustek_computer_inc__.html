Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

ASUS is in acute distress with memory costs doubling to 30% of BOM, forcing price hikes that IDC warns will disproportionately hurt smaller OEMs versus Dell/HP. With motherboard shipments collapsing 33% and margin compression visible, ASUS needs immediate planning transformation to dynamically rebalance procurement, SKU mix, and pricing. Strong Accenture buying signal as crisis is public, quantified, and time-sensitive.
