Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

CVS is in active transformation with new CEO David Joyner (Oct 2024), a $2bn cost reduction program, and 1,170 store closures creating urgent need for supply chain reinvention. The 13.3% pharmacy margin decline and PBM reimbursement pressure combined with existing Kinaxis platform presence creates a high-probability window for Accenture to embed planning modernization and AI analytics into their restructuring roadmap.
