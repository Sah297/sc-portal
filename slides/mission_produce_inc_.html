Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Mission Produce is in acute distress following a catastrophic ERP failure that destroyed $22.2m in gross profit and operational capability. The company publicly acknowledged 'significant challenges' and spent $3.8m on emergency consulting to stabilize operations. With revenue now at $1.39bn and ongoing working capital pressures, Mission needs immediate planning transformation to prevent future disruptions. This is a classic buying signal: crisis-driven urgency, proven capability gaps, executive visibility, and clear ROI path.
