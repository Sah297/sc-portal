Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Intel is in acute crisis mode with publicly acknowledged hand-to-mouth operations and a CFO-confirmed 6-month constrained supply window. The company beat earnings by optimizing product mix for margin but left $1.4bn in unmet demand on the table in Q1 2026 alone. This creates urgent need for sophisticated allocation planning capabilities that Accenture can deploy rapidly via o9 + PaaS model while the constraint window is active.

Signals to Lead With

→ Explicit CFO acknowledgment of hand-to-mouth operations — CFO stated on Q1 2026 call that Intel operates hand-to-mouth getting products from fab to customers with no buffer inventory and 6-month recovery timeline.
