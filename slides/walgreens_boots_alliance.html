Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Walgreens represents an urgent, high-value opportunity with CEO Tim Wentworth leading a multi-year turnaround requiring immediate supply chain transformation. The company is actively executing a $1bn cost reduction program while managing the most complex footprint optimization in retail pharmacy history, creating a clear mandate for sophisticated planning capabilities and analytics to avoid destroying $280m+ in inventory value during network rationalization.
