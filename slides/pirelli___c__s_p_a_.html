Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Pirelli faces a perfect storm requiring immediate supply chain transformation: inventory bloat from tariff hedging, 250bps forex headwinds, and governance paralysis blocking strategic decisions. With adjusted EBIT margin targets at risk (lower end of guidance) and mitigation plans urgently needed, this is a high-urgency consulting opportunity. The company already invested in Kinaxis but lacks operational maturity to leverage it amid crisis.
