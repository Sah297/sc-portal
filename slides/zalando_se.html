Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Zalando is in the midst of a major operational transformation, closing Brieselang and Erfurt fulfillment centers by mid-2026 while consolidating to 14 automated sites post-About You acquisition. With €100m annual EBIT at stake, 2,700 employees affected, and inventory growing faster than revenue (14.9% vs 7.3%), the company faces critical planning challenges during this network restructuring. The timing is ideal for Accenture to deliver integrated planning capabilities on o9 Solutions to orchestrate the transition.

Signals to Lead With

→ Active network restructuring with hard deadline — Brieselang closes June 2026, Erfurt closes September 2026, requiring immediate planning transformation to manage transition across 14 consolidated sites.
→ Quantified EBIT improvement target — €100m annual operating profit improvement targeted from logistics network optimization, creating clear ROI justification for planning investment.
→ Inventory efficiency deterioration — Q2 2025 inventory growth of 14.9% outpaced 7.3% revenue growth, with gross margin down 80bps, signaling urgent need for demand and allocation optimization.
→ Dual B2B/B2C complexity escalating — ZEOS platform expanding to 10 additional markets while integrating About You's SCAYLE unit, requiring unified planning across business models and 29 markets.

How Accenture Can Help

• Planning Opportunities: S&OP redesign to synchronize B2C and B2B demand across consolidated network during About You integration and facility closures.
• Platform (o9 Solutions): o9 Solutions enables unified planning across Zalando's 14-site network, integrating ZEOS B2B fulfillment with B2C operations and real-time inventory optimization.
• Planning as a Service: PaaS accelerates go-live during 2026 restructuring transition while building internal planning capability for post-merger ecosystem.
• AI & Analytics: AI agents optimize markdown timing and multi-echelon allocation across automated fulfillment centers to prevent inventory bloat and protect margins.
