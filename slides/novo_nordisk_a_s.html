Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Novo Nordisk's February 2026 earnings warning represents a watershed moment for the company after years of hypergrowth. The -5% to -13% sales and profit decline guidance, combined with 370bps margin compression and thousands of layoffs, signals an urgent need for supply chain transformation to navigate unprecedented pricing pressure and patent expiries. With the company in active restructuring mode and facing its worst year on record (stock down 50% in 2025), this is a prime buying window for large-scale planning and optimization engagements.
