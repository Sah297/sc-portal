Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Grupo Bimbo faces acute margin pressure from transportation security costs (up to 10% of OpEx industry-wide) while simultaneously investing heavily in North American value chain transformation that compressed operating margin 270 bps in FY2024. With 54,000 distribution routes, MX$270bn in logistics costs across the Mexican food sector, and a 47% operating income decline in their largest region (46.4% of sales), the company needs immediate supply chain planning capabilities to recover profitability while managing complexity across 35 countries and 227 bakeries.
