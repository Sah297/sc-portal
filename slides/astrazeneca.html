Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

AstraZeneca's March 2026 Clean Heat Programme launch represents a funded, executive-sponsored initiative with defined supplier targets (95% by 2025) and regulatory urgency (CBAM, SEC rules). With gross margin declining, Scope 3 rising 18.6%, and 98% of emissions sitting in supply chain, this is a Board-level priority requiring immediate planning capability upgrades. The company is already a Kinaxis RapidResponse customer, creating a low-friction expansion opportunity with high strategic value.
