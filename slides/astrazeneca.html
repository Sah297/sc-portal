Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

AstraZeneca faces a critical window to address supply chain vulnerabilities as it pursues its Ambition 2030 ($80bn revenue target) while managing Scope 3 emissions that rose 18.6% since 2019. With $2.2bn capex investment in manufacturing expansion (up 50% in FY2025), geographic API supplier concentration, and $5.7bn inventory (212 days), the company needs integrated planning transformation to de-risk growth, meet net-zero commitments (50% Scope 3 reduction by 2030), and release working capital. Strong buying signal: major manufacturing investments underway require planning platform modernization now.
