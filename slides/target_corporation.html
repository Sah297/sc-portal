Why this is a buying signal for Accenture

Target represents a high-urgency opportunity with clear buying signals: negative comps for multiple consecutive quarters (-2.6% FY2025), explicit public commitment (March 2025) to modernize inventory management systems with AI-powered technology, and ongoing consumable in-stock issues driving customer attrition. The company's use of Blue Yonder platform creates natural entry point for Accenture planning reinvention work, while $104.8bn scale and 27.9% margin compression provide substantial financial justification.
