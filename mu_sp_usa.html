Key Procurement Issues

Extreme Procurement Cycle Times Impeding R&D Velocity

6-9 month RFP cycles delayed critical R&D procurement, creating scientist bypass behaviors and ~85,000 hours/year of manual effort wasted. Post-AI implementation reduced to <30 days, enabling 10x volume increase with 50% fewer resources.

Senior executives publicly admitted to bypassing procurement due to excessive timelines. The company processed $1B+ through AI platform in first year, far exceeding projections, while insourcing work previously outsourced.

Capabilities Needed

AI-Powered Source-to-Contract Procurement Operating Model Redesign Process Standardization & Automation

Fragmented Systems and Email-Based Workflows Constraining Productivity

Manual, email-based workflows across disconnected systems consumed 85,000+ hours annually in non-value-add activities. Approval cycles stretched across days instead of hours, reducing procurement's strategic value.

Transformation launched June 2024 focused on eliminating fragmented workflows and standardizing processes. Implementation of unified platform with AI chatbot and intelligent agents dramatically reduced clicks and manual interventions.

Capabilities Needed

Intelligent Procurement Platform (SynOps/ATLAS) Process Mining & Optimization Change Management for Agile Adoption

Data Quality Barriers Delaying AI/Digital Transformation ROI

Imperfect data historically cited as barrier to AI adoption across industry (only 10% of CFOs trust data quality). BMS overcame by implementing 'iterate while cleaning' approach, achieving 95%+ cycle time reduction despite data imperfections.

Company deliberately moved forward with AI without waiting for perfect data, challenging industry conventional wisdom. Approach enabled rapid deployment and measurable results within 90 days of go-live.

Capabilities Needed

AI/ML Data Readiness Acceleration Agile Procurement Transformation Data Governance for Continuous Improvement

Where the value lies

Primary value lies in scaling AI transformation beyond sourcing to full P2P cycle, unlocking $400-800M in SG&A/COGS savings through category optimization, and embedding supplier risk intelligence to protect critical R&D and manufacturing supply chains. Accenture can accelerate BMS's journey from Medium to High procurement maturity through operating model redesign and enterprise SynOps deployment.
